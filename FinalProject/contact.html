About Us

We are a team of four, all currently enrolled in Sonoma State University on our last semester for our bachelors in Comupter Science. This website is our senior capstone project for graduation. This project is soley non-profit.

FAQ

Q: Does Streamline cost money?

A: No, we do not even ask you to make an account to use our services. The streaming services we feature however, like Hulu, do require a subscription. We are in no means a way to access free movies and TV shows, but rather a way to find new TV shows and movies appropriate for your children based on the services you are already subscribed too.

Q: What age should children be to use this site?

A: Our target age is from 5-10, but many people may be interested in what we have to offer. We even feature TV shows that teens may be interested for a little bit of nostalgia, like Spongebob and Finding Nemo!

Q: Is Streamline a streaming service?

A: No, we are simply an interface to allow searching across multiple streaming services for child freindly content.
